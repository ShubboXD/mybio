How to Play

  • Select two number tiles, then choose an operation (+, ‚àí, √ó, √∑) to combine them.
  • The result replaces the tiles you selected.
  • Keep combining numbers until you reach the target value.
  • You can only use each tile once, and all operations must result in whole numbers.
  • Complete levels to earn points and advance to harder challenges.
  • The fewer moves you use, the higher your score!
  • You earn 1 hint per level for levels 1-7, 2 hints for levels 8-15, and 3 hints for levels 16+.
  • Unused hints give bonus points when completing a level.
  • "Reset Board" will be available if the target becomes impossible to reach.
  • "Next Game" is only available when you successfully reach the target.
  • The game has 24 levels with increasing difficulty - can you master them all?
